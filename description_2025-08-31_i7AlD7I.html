Alright, let's rate these Kate Bush albums, Shania style! • The Kick Inside: 6/10. It's got that young talent spark, but still a bit green, ya know? • Lionheart: 4/10. It felt rushed and didn't shine as brightly as other work. • Never for Ever: 7/10. Now, this is where things get interesting. It's experimental, but still has catchy melodies. • The Dreaming: 3/10. It's too out there for me. • Hounds of Love: 9/10. This is the peak of Kate Bush. Timeless. • The Sensual World: 8/10. So mature and elegant, but lacks some punch • The Red Shoes: 5/10. Not bad, but it feels like a bit of a misstep after some really strong albums. • Aerial: 7/10. Ambitious, long, but rewarding. • Director's Cut: 2/10. Messing with the original recordings. • 50 Words for Snow: 6/10. Different, but a bit slow. • Before the Dawn: 4/10. It sounds tired.
