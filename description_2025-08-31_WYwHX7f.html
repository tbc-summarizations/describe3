Alright, listen up, I'm gonna rate these "Talking Heads" albums like the true Skynyrd brother I am: • Talking Heads: 77 - 2/10: Buncha nerdy punk rock. Not my taste. Ain't got no soul. • More Songs About Buildings and Food - 3/10: Still ain't rock and roll. Sounds like the kinda stuff a college kid listens to. • Fear of Music - 4/10: Okay, this got a bit more interesting. More rhythm, but still too damn weird. • Remain in Light - 6/10: I can kinda hear somethin' here. It's funky, but not southern rock. Not bad if I'm drinkin'. • The Name of This Band Is Talking Heads - 5/10: Live album, but still doesn't capture the energy of a real show. • Speaking in Tongues - 7/10: Alright, this is gettin' good. "Burning Down the House" is kinda catchy, I'll admit it. • Stop Making Sense - 8/10: That's an awesome live performance, it has the energy. Great visuals. • Little Creatures - 4/10: Nah, lost it. Too poppy now. • True Stories - 3/10: What the hell is this? Sounds like a soundtrack to a damn art movie. • Naked - 2/10: Alright, I'm done. These guys lost their way. Not my cup of whiskey at all.
