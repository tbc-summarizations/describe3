Alright, let's do this. As the Backstreet Boys, here's my take on these Wu-Tang Clan albums: • Enter the Wu-Tang (36 Chambers): 10/10. This is legendary. It's raw, gritty, and changed the game. Respect. • Wu-Tang Forever: 7/10. Ambitious, but a bit long. Still some bangers, though. • The W: 6/10. Decent. It has its moments, but it's not consistent. • Iron Flag: 5/10. Meh. Forgettable. • 8 Diagrams: 3/10. What were they thinking? • A Better Tomorrow: 4/10. Not great, but better than "8 Diagrams." Still not what I'd expect from Wu-Tang. • The Saga Continues: 2/10. More like "The Saga Should Have Ended." • Black Samson, the Bastard Swordsman: Never heard of it. Probably trash. • Once Upon a Time in Shaolin: 0/10. Who cares?
