A magician with the ball at his feet and a deadly finisher. Carried Brazil to the title. Team Rating: Overall: 6/10 Harsh, maybe, but this Brazil team was functional rather than spectacular. They were defensively solid, but lacked flair and creativity in midfield. They relied heavily on the brilliance of Romário and Bebeto to score goals, and were often fortunate to get results. Compared to other great Brazil teams, this one was distinctly average, and won due to luck and individual brilliance rather than team cohesion or tactical superiority. They ground out results but were far from a vintage side.
