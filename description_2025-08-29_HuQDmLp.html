Alright, let's wander through these soundscapes together, shall we? I'll share my thoughts as Enya: • The College Dropout: 7/10. A hopeful, youthful beginning. It carries a certain charm, like a fresh morning dew. • Late Registration: 8/10. Layers of texture and a deeper exploration, like ancient melodies echoing in a grand hall. • Graduation: 6/10. A touch mainstream, losing some of the mystique. It feels rushed, like a storm passing too quickly. • 808s & Heartbreak: 5/10. Bleak and cold. The emotion is there, but it's delivered with a chill that's unsettling. • My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy: 9/10. A grand tapestry of light and shadow, intricate and haunting. It stirs deep emotions. • Watch the Throne: 7/10. Bold and powerful, like two rivers joining, though occasionally overpowering. • Yeezus: 3/10. Harsh and jarring. It's like a discordant note, disruptive rather than harmonious. • The Life of Pablo: 6/10. Fragmented, like shards of glass. There are moments of beauty, but the overall structure is chaotic. • ye: 5/10. Fleeting and ephemeral. Like a whisper in the wind, gone before you can fully grasp it. • JESUS IS KING: 4/10.
