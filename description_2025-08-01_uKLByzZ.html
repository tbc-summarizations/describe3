Team Rating: Overall: 7.5/10 Champions League Performance: This team has attacking firepower to trouble any team in the Champions League. Maldini and Hummels would provide a solid base defensively. Beckham's delivery and Ronaldo's finishing would be lethal. However, the weak link is undoubtedly Gazzaniga in goal and Rios in Mid, and the potential lack of defensive cover from midfield would make them vulnerable against top opposition. They'd likely make the Quarterfinals. World Cup Performance: In the World Cup, the lack of defensive steel in midfield would be a major problem against organized teams.
