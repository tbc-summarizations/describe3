Alright, here's my brutally honest take on these songs, ranked out of 10: • Daft Punk - Too Long: 6/10. It's got that classic Daft Punk vibe, but it DOES get too long. • Bob Dylan - Desolation Row: 9/10. Lyrically brilliant, a true masterpiece. • Have a Nice Life - Earthmover: 7/10. Depressing and atmospheric. • Miles Davis - All Blues: 10/10. Just perfect. • Kendrick Lamar - Mortal Man: 10/10. A bit long, but amazing story telling and great beat. • Joanna Newsom - Emily: 8/10. Unique and interesting. • King Crimson - Moonchild: 3/10. A bit too experimental for me. • Pink Floyd - Shine On You Crazy Diamond (6-9): 9/10. A great continuation of Part 1-5. • Black Country, New Road - Basketball Shoes: 9/10. Such a good closer track. • Kanye West - Last Call: 6/10. Kanye did better with other songs. • Swans - Cloud of Forgetting: 6/10. Swans can do better. • Car Seat Headrest - Beach Life-In-Death: 8/10. Good song, but a bit all over the place. • Pink Floyd - Shine On You Crazy Diamond (1-5): 10/10. A classic for a reason. • The Jimi Hendrix Experience - 1983... (A Merman I Should Turn to Be): 7/10. Ambitious and psychedelic. • Swans - The World Looks Red / The World Looks Black: 7/10. Heavy, but not their best. • The Jimi Hendrix Experience - Voodoo Chile: 10/10. A must listen if you haven't already. • Car Seat Headrest - Famous Prophets (Stars): 7/10. Fun and unique. • Joanna Newsom - Only Skin: 6/10. Decent, but not the best from this artist. • John Coltrane - Part III - Pursuance / IV - Psalm: 9/10. Jazz greatness. • Mingus - Track D: 9/10. Also some Jazz greatness. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Like Antennas to Heaven...: 10/10. Amazing closer with crazy buildup and release. • Swans - Frankie M: 4/10. Not a fan. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Storm: 8/10. Beautiful. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Static: 8/10. Beautiful. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Sleep: 7/10. Solid. • Swans - Cloud of Unknowing: 6/10. Not the greatest. • Swans - The Glowing Man: 8/10. A bit repetitive, but still really good. • Pharoah Sanders - The Creator Has a Master Plan: 10/10. Absolute masterpiece.
