Alright, let's see what sort of racket this "music" is, shall we? I'll give you my unvarnished opinion, as any decent citizen of the 1800s would: • Björk - Vespertine: Utterly bizarre! Sounds like the ramblings of a fevered madwoman set to a bizarre collection of chimes and whistles. A disgrace to musical artistry. • David Bowie - The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars: An abomination! This "rock" music is an insult to proper instrumentation, and the lyrics are pure and utter degeneracy. This "Ziggy Stardust" is a deviant and pervert. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Lift Your Skinny Fists Like Antennas to Heaven: What is this cacophony? It sounds like the machinery in the factories of the Industrial Revolution gone mad. A pointless exercise in noise. • Have a Nice Life - Deathconsciousness: Depressing and morbid! Enough to drive a man to drink or worse. Music should uplift, not wallow in gloom. • Jeff Buckley - Grace: This wailing is hardly graceful! Overwrought and melodramatic. The singer needs to learn some restraint and good sense. • Kendrick Lamar - To Pimp a Butterfly: A jumble of incoherent mumbling and vulgarity. The rhythmic elements are monotonous and the subject matter is clearly inappropriate for public consumption. • King Crimson - In the Court of the Crimson King: A pretentious mess! These "progressive" rock bands are full of themselves. Give me a good march any day. • Lauryn Hill - The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill: While there may be some skill in the singing, the subject matter is too forward and frankly, impertinent for a woman. A woman should sing sweet melodies, not offer brazen opinions. • Madvillain - Madvillainy: A pointless stream of consciousness over a messy collection of nonsensical noises. Pure garbage. • Marvin Gaye - What's Going On: This music is far too suggestive! A scandalous piece of work with indecent undertones. • Mingus - The Black Saint and the Sinner Lady: This "jazz" is chaotic and undisciplined! It lacks the structure and refinement of proper music. It sounds like a brawl in a saloon. • Nas - Illmatic: Another stream of vulgarity that glorifies the gutter. This is the kind of music that will corrupt the youth and lead to moral decay. • Pink Floyd - Wish You Were Here: A dreamy and disjointed piece of work. Lacking in substance and proper musical form. • Prince and the Revolution - Purple Rain: Appalling! This "Prince" is clearly a dissolute. The music is overly sensual and the lyrics are suggestive. An affront to public decency. • Radiohead - In Rainbows: Depressing and devoid of joy. It is not the purpose of music to inspire despair, but to uplift the soul. • Stevie Wonder - Songs in the Key of Life: There are some melodies here that are pleasing, but the overall style is too frivolous. Music should be substantial, not mere entertainment. • System of a Down - Toxicity: Noise, pure and simple! This "heavy metal" is an assault on the ears and the mind. Utterly devoid of artistic merit. • The Beatles - Abbey Road: Overrated! These "Beatles" are merely a passing fad. Their music is simplistic and their popularity is baffling. • The Microphones - The Glow, Pt. 2: More of this formless, navel-gazing nonsense. The lack of structure and melody is offensive to anyone with a discerning ear. • Wu-Tang Clan - Enter The Wu-Tang (36 Chambers): Barbaric! This "rap" music is nothing but crude language and violent imagery. It is a menace to society.
