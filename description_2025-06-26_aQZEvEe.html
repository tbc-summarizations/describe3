Alright, here's the brutally honest rating of Dutch clubs, as requested: • Ajax: 10/10 - No questions asked. They are THE club in the Netherlands. History, trophies, youth development...the best in the country and easily one of the best in the world. • Almere City: 3/10 - They exist. That's about it. Barely worth mentioning in the grand scheme of Dutch football. • AZ: 7/10 - A solid club that punches above its weight. They've had periods of challenging the big boys, but they ultimately lack the resources and consistently needed to truly compete at the top. • FC Utrecht: 6/10 - A decent team with a loyal fanbase. They're good enough to stay relevant, but never really threaten for major honors. • Feyenoord: 9/10 - One of the 'big three' and a historical powerhouse. They have a passionate fanbase and a strong tradition, but have been in Ajax's shadow for too long. • Fortuna Sittard: 4/10 - A small club, often struggling to stay in the Eredivisie. • Go Ahead Eagles: 5/10 - A club with a rich history, but these days, they're mostly just trying to survive in the top flight. • Groningen: 5/10 - They've had their moments, but have declined a lot, they're decent but nothing special. • Heerenveen: 6/10 - A team with a reputation for developing young talents, but they are too inconsistent to really make an impact. • Heracles Almelo: 4/10 - Another small club that bounces between the Eredivisie and the Eerste Divisie. • NAC Breda: 4/10 - A club with a great fanbase, but on the field, they're not much to write home about. • NEC: 5/10 - Similar to NAC Breda, good fanbase but not consistent enough to achieve anything significant. • PEC Zwolle: 4/10 - A club with a relatively small profile, often fighting relegation. • PSV: 9/10 - The only club in the country that can realistically challenge Ajax, they have a rich history, but have struggled to maintain consistency. • RKC Waalwijk: 3/10 - Another small club. • Sparta Rotterdam: 5/10 - One of the oldest clubs in the Netherlands, but their glory days are long gone. • Twente: 7/10 - They won the title once, but have had financial issues and haven't been able to compete consistently at the top since then. • Willem II: 4/10 - Usually struggling at the bottom.
