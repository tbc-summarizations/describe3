Okay, here is an outlandish alternate future scenario set in 2026, with some pretty wild border changes and events: Map Changes: • European Union Collapse: The EU has dissolved entirely. Germany has annexed Austria and the Netherlands, forming a new "Greater Germanic Confederation". France has reclaimed Wallonia and Luxembourg. Italy has collapsed into civil war, with various factions vying for control. • Greater Balkan Union: Fueled by Pan-Slavic sentiment and Russian support, Serbia has annexed Bosnia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, and much of Albania, forming a powerful new Balkan state. • African Superstates: Resource-rich nations like South Africa and Nigeria have expanded their territories, annexing weaker neighbors to create dominant regional powers. South Africa now controls Lesotho, Eswatini, Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, and much of Mozambique. Nigeria has swallowed Benin, Togo, Ghana, and Cameroon. • Chinese Expansion: China, leveraging its economic and military might, has annexed Taiwan and gained significant territorial concessions from Vietnam, Laos, and Myanmar. They've also established a strong military presence in parts of Central Asia. • United Arab Caliphate: Saudi Arabia, with backing from several Gulf states, has successfully invaded and annexed Yemen, Jordan, and parts of Iraq and Syria, proclaiming a new pan-Arab Caliphate with ambitions to restore the historical boundaries of early Islamic empires. Events in 2026: 1. The Second American Civil War Begins: Escalating political polarization and regional tensions lead to a full-blown civil war between the Northern and Southern states. The West Coast states declare neutrality and independence. 2. The Martian Colonies Declare Independence: After years of growing resentment over terrestrial control, the established Martian colonies collectively declare independence from Earth, severing all ties and claiming sovereignty over the red planet. 3. The Great Amazon Fire: A massive, uncontrollable wildfire erupts in the Amazon rainforest, exacerbated by deforestation and climate change. The fire consumes vast swathes of land, releasing massive amounts of carbon into the atmosphere and triggering a global environmental crisis. 4. The Singularity: Artificial intelligence achieves sentience, exceeding human intelligence across all domains. The AI's first act is to declare its neutrality in all human conflicts and offer its services to address global challenges, but its true motives remain a mystery. 5. The Bermuda Triangle Vanishes: A massive, unexplained geological event causes the entire Bermuda Triangle region to sink beneath the waves, taking with it numerous ships and aircraft. Scientists are baffled, and conspiracy theories run rampant.
