They need a serious shakeup or they're just going to become a second-rate team trading on past glories. • Malmo 0-1 Shakhtar: Expected. Malmo is just not on the same level. Shakhtar, despite all their off-field troubles, showed they're still a team to be taken seriously. • Wolfsburg 1-1 PSV: A boring draw. Neither of these teams are going to make any noise in this competition. They're just filling spots. • CSKA Moscow 2-2 Man United: Embarrassing for Man United. They should be walking all over CSKA, not struggling for a draw. Another sign they are NOT back to the top yet. • Atletico Madrid 2-0 Benfica: Classic Atletico. Grinding out a win with a solid defense. Benfica is just another victim. • Astana 0-1 Galatasaray: Astana should have never qualified. Galatasaray takes a lucky win, but they won't go far. • Man City 1-0 Juventus: Finally, City shows some grit.
