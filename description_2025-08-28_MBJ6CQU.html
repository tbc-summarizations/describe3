Alright, buckle up, because I'm about to tear this list apart. Here's my brutally honest take: • Another Star - Stevie Wonder: Solid start, can't argue with Stevie. But there are so much songs starting with letter A this pick is pretty noob. • Bring Da Ruckus - Wu-Tang Clan: Good choice, raw energy. Definitely a high-tier B song. • Carry That Weight - The Beatles: Overrated Beatles song. Should have gone with "Come Together". • Dogs - Pink Floyd: Yes! Absolute masterpiece. No debate. • Earthmover - Have A Nice Life: Edgy, but ultimately pretentious. Overstays its welcome. • Fourth of July - Sufjan Stevens: Great song, but way too depressing. Not enjoyable to listen to repeatedly. • Gangsta's Paradise - Coolio: Nostalgic, but musically basic. Overplayed, and not even Coolio's best work. • House of Balloons / Glass Table Girls - The Weeknd: Great choice. Peak Weeknd. • I'm God - Clams Casino: Amazing beat. • Jigsaw Falling Into Place - Radiohead: Not even top 5 Radiohead songs. • Korowód - Marek Grechuta: Fine, if you're into obscure Polish music. But hardly the best "K" song of all time. • LONG SEASON - Fishmans: Never heard of it, but I am willing to try. • Master of Puppets - Metallica: Undeniable. Legendary. End of discussion. • N.Y. State of Mind - Nas: Absolutely. This is perfection. • On GP - Death Grips: Intense and groundbreaking. Good choice. • PRIDE. - Kendrick Lamar: Excellent. Kendrick always delivers. • Question! - System of a Down: Solid SOAD track, but there are better options. • Rigor Mormist - Laura Shigihara: Okay, this is straight up garbage. Why? • Starless - King Crimson: Beautiful, atmospheric, but can be a bit boring for some. • Transdermal Celebration - Ween: Bizarre and brilliant. I approve. • Under Pressure - Queen & David Bowie: Iconic, powerful. No complaints. • Vigilante - Willie Colón & Héctor Lavoe: Great choice if you're a salsa fan. • Wonderful Life - Black: Depressing and beautiful, but there are so much better. • Xtal - Aphex Twin: A classic. Essential electronic music. • You Shook Me All Night Long - AC/DC: Total banger. Can't argue with this. • Zombie - The Cranberries: Overplayed radio hit. Pass. Overall, a mixed bag. Some excellent choices, some questionable ones, and some that are just plain awful. Needs work.
