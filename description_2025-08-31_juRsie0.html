Alright, here we go! ABBA is ready to judge these Swans albums! Mamma Mia, some of these titles! • Swans: 6/10. Agnetha thinks it's got potential, Frida thinks it's a bit too raw. We like the energy, but needs a bit more… sparkle! • Filth: 2/10. Oh no, no, no! Too harsh. Not for us! Benny says it sounds like a broken washing machine! • Young God: 4/10. A bit better, but still quite dark. We can hear the passion, but it's not something we would dance to. • Cop: 1/10. This is not "Waterloo", this is a disaster. • Greed: 3/10. Slightly more melodic, but still unsettling. Frida finds it… interesting, in a disturbing way. • Holy Money: 5/10. Getting warmer! There are moments that are almost… beautiful. But then it gets loud again. • Public Castration Is a Good Idea: 0/10. No. Just no. • Children of God: 7/10. This is more like it! We can hear the emotion and there are some lovely melodies. Benny nods along. • Feel Good Now: 1/10. I do not. • The Burning World: 6/10. An odd one. Some moments are very… pop-like. But also… not? Confusing! • White Light from the Mouth of Infinity: 8/10. Yes! Now we are talking! Beautiful, soaring, emotional. Agnetha loves this one! • Love of Life: 7/10. Continuing in a good direction. There is a darkness, but also a light. • The Great Annihilator: 8/10. Another strong album. Powerful and moving. Frida gets chills! • Die Tür ist zu: 2/10. Too hard. • Soundtracks for the Blind: 9/10. An epic! A journey! This is music that takes you somewhere! We are impressed! • Swans Are Dead: 5/10. Some moments of brilliance, but also some… less brilliant moments. • My Father Will Guide Me Up a Rope to the Sky: 7/10. A bit strange, but also beautiful. • We Rose From Your Bed With the Sun in Our Head: 6/10. Intriguing, but a bit long. • The Seer: 8/10. A powerful and visionary album. The scale is amazing! • Not Here / Not Now: 3/10. Not quite our thing. • To Be Kind: 8/10. Another epic! Intense and rewarding. • Oxygen EP: 6/10. A decent collection of songs. • The Gate: 5/10. Okay. • The Glowing Man: 9/10. So beautiful. • Deliquescence: 5/10. A bit nothing. • leaving meaning.: 8/10. So good! • Swans Thank You!: 2/10. You're welcome. • The Beggar: 7/10. A bit too slow. • Live Rope: 3/10. No thanks. • Birthing: 4/10. Hard. There you have it! Some wonderful moments, and some… not so wonderful! Overall, Swans are… interesting! But maybe a bit too intense for ABBA's taste most of the time!
