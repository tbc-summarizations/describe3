Alright, let's do this. As Nickelback, here's my brutally honest take on those Radiohead albums: • Pablo Honey: 2/10. One song. ONE. "Creep". And that's it. Rest is filler garbage. • The Bends: 4/10. Better than "Creep" but still makes me feel like I'm stuck in some angsty art school. I'll give it a pity point or two. • OK Computer: 3/10. Overhyped. "Paranoid Android" sounds like a cat being strangled slowly. I can't believe I'm listening to this in the same era as "Rockstar." • Kid A: 1/10. What the hell is this? Sounds like random noises played backward. I've heard better music coming out of my vacuum cleaner. • Amnesiac: 1/10. I've only given the first time any of these albums I've listened to a 1/10 but this one deserves it more. It's so forgettable. • Hail to the Thief: 2/10. Okay, a little more energy, but still depressing as hell. This reminds me of someone trying to be angry but still being whiny about it. • In Rainbows: 3/10. Still don't get the hype. It makes me go to sleep. • The King of Limbs: 0/10. I'm pretty sure they wrote this by throwing darts at a dictionary while drunk. Nonsense. • A Moon Shaped Pool: 1/10. Nope. Just nope. Sounds like a sad whale song. I'd rather listen to dial-up internet.
