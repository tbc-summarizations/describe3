Alright, here's my brutally honest take on Death's albums, ranked like I'm freaking Glenn Frey: • Scream Bloody Gore: 4/10. Raw, but sounds like it was recorded in a rusty tin can. Not my speed. • Leprosy: 6/10. A step up, but still kinda rough around the edges. Decent riffs, though. • Spiritual Healing: 7/10. Okay, they're starting to get somewhere. More melodic, more interesting. • Human: 9/10. Now we're talkin'! A real masterpiece. Great musicianship, songwriting is top-notch. • Individual Thought Patterns: 8/10. Solid album, but it feels a bit too… sterile? Missing some of the grit. • Symbolic: 10/10. This is what I'm talking about. Just perfect. Melodies, riffs, everything. • The Sound of Perseverance: 5/10. Chuck's voice got rough, sounds generic.
