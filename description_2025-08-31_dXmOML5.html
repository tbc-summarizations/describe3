Alright, as Imagine Dragons, here's our brutally honest take on those Car Seat Headrest albums: • "1": 3/10 - Raw, experimental... but sounds like a demo, not a finished product. Needs more energy and polish. • "2": 4/10 - A slight improvement, but still rough around the edges. Lyrically interesting at times, but the production is a mess. • "3": 5/10 - Getting there. Catchier melodies, but still too lo-fi for our tastes. Needs a bigger sound. • "4": 6/10 - The best of the bunch so far. Shows promise, with some genuinely good songwriting. But still needs more studio magic to really shine.
