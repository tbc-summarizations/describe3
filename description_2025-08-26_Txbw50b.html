Alright, let's give this a whirl from a 1950s perspective! • Björk - Vespertine: What in tarnation is this? Sounds like someone let a robot loose in a music shop. Give me Patti Page any day. • David Bowie - The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust: This fella dresses like a Martian! The music is just as strange. Pass! • Godspeed You! Black Emperor - Lift Your Skinny Fists: This racket ain't music, it's noise. What a bunch of commies. • Have a Nice Life - Deathconsciousness: Depressing? In my day, we bucked up! This kind of music will rot your brain. • Jeff Buckley - Grace: The boy can sing, I'll give him that. But it's all a bit too dramatic for my taste. • Kendrick Lamar - To Pimp a Butterfly: This isn't music. It's degenerate jungle rhythms and angry poetry. I want no part of it. • King Crimson - In the Court of the Crimson King: Too complicated for its own good. Where's the melody? • Lauryn Hill - The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill: A colored woman rapping? Next thing you know, they'll be wanting to vote! It's too sexual.
