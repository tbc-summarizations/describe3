Alright, here's how the Ramones would rate those Pink Floyd albums, no holding back: • The Piper at the Gates of Dawn: 5/10 - Syd Barrett's a loon, but there's some catchy stuff. • A Saucerful of Secrets: 4/10 - Drifting into space junk. Needs more speed. • Ummagumma: 1/10 - What is this crap? Avant-garde wankery! • Atom Heart Mother: 3/10 - The title track is too damn long. • Meddle: 6/10 - "One of These Days" rocks. The rest is filler. • The Dark Side of the Moon: 7/10 - Alright, it's catchy and well-produced, but kinda pretentious. • Wish You Were Here: 6/10 - "Shine On You Crazy Diamond" drags on. Get to the point! • Animals: 5/10 - Moody and cynical. Not our style. • The Wall: 6/10 - Overblown, but has some good parts. • The Final Cut: 2/10 - Waters' solo album disguised as Pink Floyd. Depressing garbage. • A Momentary Lapse of Reason: 3/10 - Bland and generic. • The Division Bell: 3/10 - More blandness. • The Endless River: 1/10 - Pointless ambient noise. Zzzzzzzzz.
