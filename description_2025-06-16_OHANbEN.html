Alright, let's tear this Europa League "Team of the Season" apart, player by player, then the whole mess, and finally see how they'd actually perform. I'm not holding back. Individual Ratings: • Guglielmo Vicario (7/10): Solid keeper, makes some eye-catching saves. But he needs to work on command in the box and distribution. Can get exposed in high-pressure games. • Pedro Porro (6/10): Decent going forward, but defensively a liability. Offers almost nothing in terms of preventing goals. Pure wing-back, not a true defender. • Cristian Romero (7/10): Aggressive and loves a tackle. Can be reckless and give away silly fouls/penalties. Not always positionally sound. • Robin Koch (5/10): Pretty average. Nothing special. Mediocre pace, passing, and tackling. A liability. • Fredrik André Bjørkan (3/10): No pace, no stamina, no physicality, no defending ability. Just a wasted slot. • Patrick Berg (4/10): Does nothing well. Poor vision, passing and tackling. A liability. • Bruno Fernandes (8/10): Creative, scores and assists. Prone to giving the ball away with risky passes. His attitude can be toxic. • Casemiro (8/10): Defensive monster when he wants to be. Age is catching up to him, gets overrun in midfield against high-energy teams. • Rayan Cherki (6/10): Skillful. Lacks physicality and consistency. Disappears in big games. • Dominic Solanke (6/10): A decent goal scorer, lacks high-end pace and isn't particularly clinical. • Nico Williams (7/10): Fast, direct, can beat defenders. Poor decision-making and finishing. Overall Team Rating: 5/10 This team is a mess. The defense is the primary issue, with weak links throughout the backline. The midfield is better, with a decent blend of creativity and defensive bite, but lacks the legs to control a game against top-tier opponents. The attack has some flair but lacks a consistent goal-scorer. Overall, the team would be easily exposed by any well-organized, high-pressing side. How They'd Do in the Europa League: • Group Stage: Would struggle to win the group stage, but could still make it. This team has enough individual talent to beat weaker sides. • Knockout Rounds (Round of 32): Would likely be eliminated in the first knockout round. A decent team could easily exploit the many weaknesses. • Overall: This team would be lucky to reach the quarter-finals. Any team that can play a high-intensity game with good tactical awareness would crush them. They'd be a disappointment, hyped up due to a few big names but ultimately lacking the cohesion and quality to compete for the Europa League title.
