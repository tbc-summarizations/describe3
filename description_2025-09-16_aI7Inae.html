Alright, here's the brutally honest rating of the Arsenal logo: Rating: 5/10 Here's why: • Generic Shield Shape: It's just another shield. Uninspired and predictable. It's like they pulled it straight from a "create your own crest" kit. • Overused Colors: Red and white? So original. Every other sports team uses those colors. They blend in, not stand out. It's boring! • Cartoonish Cannon: The cannon looks like it belongs in a kids' cartoon. It lacks any sense of power or aggression, which is what a symbol of warfare should convey. • Bland Typography: "Arsenal" slapped on top in some plain font. No creativity there at all. It feels like an afterthought. • Lack of History/Meaning: Where's the story? What does this logo say about the club other than "we have a cannon"? It's devoid of any real substance. Overall, the Arsenal logo is safe, uninspired, and forgettable. It desperately needs a modern update with a stronger, more distinctive design that reflects the club's history and ambition. It screams mediocrity, which is a shame for a team with such a rich history.
