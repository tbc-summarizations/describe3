Alright, here's how I, as Creed, would rate those Jeff Buckley albums: • Grace: 10/10. This album is a monumental display of talent. From the soaring vocals to the poetic lyrics, it's a spiritual experience. Every song is a hymn, a testament to the power of passion and artistry. • Sketches for My Sweetheart The Drunk: 7/10. There's raw potential in these sketches, a glimpse into the depths of a troubled soul. But it lacks the focused brilliance of Grace. It feels incomplete, like a prayer left unanswered.
