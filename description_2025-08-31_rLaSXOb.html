Alright, here's what I think as Creed: • day/pop: 5/10. It's alright, but it doesn't have that raw, powerful energy that I crave. Sounds too soft, not rock enough. • night/pop: 6/10. Little darker, I can feel something real underneath but it's still trying to be something it's not. • mini mix vol. 1: 4/10. It's... a mix. Sounds like someone fiddling around, doesn't grab me. • A Little Rhythm and a Wicked Feeling: 8/10. Now we're talking! It's got a groove, a darkness, a real feeling. Finally, something with a bit of edge. • mini mix vol. 2: 3/10. Lost me again. Random noise. • Mercurial World: 9/10. This is it! The real stuff. Deep, powerful, emotional, this album really gets to the core. The sound is fantastic, you can hear the passion in it. • mini mix vol. 3: 2/10. More of that random noise. • Imaginal Disk: 7/10. It has sparks. This album is a start, it has something. It is good.
