Alright, buckle up, because here comes the brutally honest Nickelback take on OutKast's discography: • Southernplayalisticadillacmuzik: 5/10 - It's like early days, finding their sound. Still kinda rough around the edges, ya know? Sounds like something you'd hear blasting out of a busted Cadillac at a gas station. • ATLiens: 8/10 - Alright, now we're talking. They started getting experimental on this one, and I like it. Production isn't the best and lyrics are ok though. • Aquemini: 9/10 - This is where OutKast figured it out. Lyrics are good, production is amazing and very unique. • Stankonia: 7/10 - It's got some energy, some raw emotion, but it's kinda all over the place. "Ms. Jackson" is alright, but the rest is skipable. • Speakerboxxx/The Love Below: 3/10 - This is where they lost me. A bunch of nonsense. I want Rock, not this garbage. • Idlewild: 1/10 - This sounds like garbage. The production is weird and the lyrics are trash.
