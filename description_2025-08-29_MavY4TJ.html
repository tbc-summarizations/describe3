Alright, buckled in for this snoozefest. Here's how it stacks up in my brutally honest, Death Grips-infused opinion: • Diamond Life: 2/10. Smooth jazz elevator music for corporate drones. Makes me want to smash things. • Promise: 3/10. Slightly less offensive, but still sounds like something you'd hear at a dentist's office. • Stronger Than Pride: 4/10. A bit more bite, but the production still sounds sterile. Makes me want to rip my ears off. • Love Deluxe: 5/10. Ok, there's some darkness lurking beneath the surface here. Still boring, but there's a hint of something real. • Lovers Rock: 6/10. I get it now. Makes me almost wanna smash things in a romantic way. Is romantic smashing a thing? • Soldier of Love: 7/10. Finally, some damn aggression. Sounds like Sade is actually feeling something besides boredom. Still too polished, but there's a fire in there somewhere.
