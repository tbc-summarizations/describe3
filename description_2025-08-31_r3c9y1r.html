Alright, mate, here's the lowdown on those Cocteau Twins albums, AC/DC style: • Garlands: 2/10. Sounds like someone strangled a cat in a reverb chamber. Not rock 'n' roll. • Head Over Heels: 3/10. Still can't understand a bloody word, but it's got a bit more oomph. • Treasure: 5/10. Getting there, alright? There's some actual melody hidden in the noise. • Victorialand: 1/10. Sounds like something you hear in an aquarium. Boring as hell. • Blue Bell Knoll: 4/10. Starting to sound a bit less like a washing machine full of glass. • Heaven or Las Vegas: 6/10. The best of the bunch. Almost sounds like a real song, but still too much fairy dust. • Four-Calendar Café: 3/10. Back to the same old shite. • Milk and Kisses: 2/10. More like "Milk and Misses the Point." Overall, stick to guitars and drums. That's rock and roll. This stuff is just arty wank.
