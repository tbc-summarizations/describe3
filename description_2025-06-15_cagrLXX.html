Alright, buckle up, here’s my unfiltered take on these clubs, no punches pulled: • Roma: 3/5 stars. Look, they have history, they have passion, but they're not consistently among the European elite. They've had flashes of brilliance, but often underachieved given their potential. A decent club, but not a powerhouse. • Manchester United: 5/5 stars. No debate here. Dominated English football for years, won multiple Champions League titles, global brand, legends galore. They are a top tier club. • Porto: 4/5 stars. Often underestimated, Porto consistently punches above its weight in Europe. They are serial champions of the Portuguese league. They have won the Champions League twice and the Europa League twice. • Ajax: 4/5 stars. A historical giant. Cruyff, total football, multiple Champions League titles. Ajax is famous for its youth academy, bringing through so many stars for club and country. However, it is worth noting that the Dutch league is not as strong as the other big leagues. • Rangers: 3/5 stars. They dominate Scottish football and the history. They are a powerhouse of Scotland, but are overshadowed by the big leagues in Europe. • Eintracht Frankfurt: 2/5 stars. Have won the Europa League once.
