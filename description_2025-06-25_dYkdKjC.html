Alright, let's dissect this UEFA Nations League B Group 3 performance with the brutal honesty it deserves: Group Overview: Norway deserved that promotion to League A. Their performance was the most consistent, led by Erling Haaland and co, they consistently delivered, even if they had that hiccup against Austria. Austria, while a decent team, I expected better than securing a spot in the promotion play-offs only. They had some decent performances such as those of Matchday 3 and 4, but they could not edge out Norway at the top. Slovenia are just... there. Qualified to the relegation play-offs. Very mediocre overall. Can't say I'm surprised with the likes of Jan Oblak on the team. They had a few good moments, such as beating Kazakhstan, but they were outclassed. Kazakhstan, absolute embarrassment. Relegated to League C with a single point and a goal difference of -15. 0 goals scored? Pathetic. They were clearly outmatched and did not belong in this group. An utter disgrace to football. Matchday Analysis: • Matchday 1: Kazakhstan actually managed a draw against Norway. Unbelievable. Slovenia and Austria were even. • Matchday 2: Norway shows their dominance and Slovenia, well, they just beat a team that has zero goals scored. • Matchday 3: Austria demolishes Kazakhstan to show who is better and Norway did the same to Slovenia. • Matchday 4: A weird matchday for Norway since they fell to Austria 5-1 and Kazakhstan actually loses by only 1 goal. • Matchday 5: Just Austria doing Austria things. Slovenia also doing what they can against Norway, but it's a heavy defeat. • Matchday 6: Austria and Slovenia tie the match while Norway just ends this league in a heavy defeat against Kazakhstan. Overall Opinion: This group was a mix of the expected and the disappointing. Norway earned their promotion, and Kazakhstan deserved their relegation. The other teams just floated around. A relatively unexciting group overall, with only a few standout moments. Nothing exceptional to remember.
