Alright, buckle up, because here's what I think of these tracks: • Daft Punk - Too Long: More like "Too Boring." Gets repetitive faster than a toddler watching "Baby Shark." • Bob Dylan - Desolation Row: Yeah, yeah, Bob, we get it, the world is messed up. Can you at least try to make it sound interesting? Sounds like a whiny hobo. • Have a Nice Life - Earthmover: Depressing garbage. It's the soundtrack to staring at the ceiling and contemplating the futility of existence. Emo garbage. • Miles Davis - All Blues: Smooth jazz? More like smooth boredom. Perfect music for falling asleep during a wine and cheese party. • Kendrick Lamar - Mortal Man: Pretentious rambling. The conversation with Tupac at the end is cringe-worthy. • Joanna Newsom - Emily: Her voice is nails on a chalkboard. This song is what I imagine it would sound like if a woodland creature was slowly being tortured. • King Crimson - Moonchild: Space is cool and everything, but I didn't need this much filler. The only thing that could be longer than this song would be the space this takes up in my brain. • Pink Floyd - Shine On You Crazy Diamond (6-9): Overrated and self-indulgent. Gets old pretty quick. • Black Country, New Road - Basketball Shoes: This song is so long I could fall in love, start a family, and then hear my grandchildren saying "Are we there yet?" before it ends. • Kanye West - Last Call: A self-aggrandizing autobiography set to a beat. At least he admits he's full of himself. • Swans - Cloud of Forgetting: You'll forget you were even listening to it 5 minutes after it ends. • Car Seat Headrest - Beach Life-In-Death: What? • Pink Floyd - Shine On You Crazy Diamond (1-5): Pink Floyd really milking it with this one. • The Jimi Hendrix Experience - 1983... (A Merman I Should Turn to Be): Jimi, I love you, but this track is way too long and meanders like a stoned goldfish. I'm not sure what I'm more confused about: the plot or the lack of it. • Swans - The World Looks Red / The World Looks Black: Yeah, and this song sounds monochromatic, too. • The Jimi Hendrix Experience - Voodoo Chile: This makes me want to perform some voodoo on my ears to stop listening to it. • Car Seat Headrest - Famous Prophets (Stars): More like "Infamous Bore-phets." • Joanna Newsom - Only Skin: Her voice is even worse in this one. • John Coltrane - Part III - Pursuance / IV - Psalm: Too jazzy. Way too jazzy for its own good. • Mingus - Track D: What? • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Like Antennas to Heaven...: This song is so long and meandering, it's like the musical equivalent of reading a phone book in another language. • Swans - Frankie M: "Frankie M" stands for "Frankly, More of this?" • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Storm: More like a drizzle. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Static: Fitting title. • Godspeed You Black Emperor! - Sleep: I think I will! • Swans - Cloud of Unknowing: At least this song admits what it's doing to you. • Swans - The Glowing Man: More like the agonizingly slow-glowing man. • Pharoah Sanders - The Creator Has a Master Plan: The creator's plan obviously involved making this song last forever.
