Alright, here's how we, Maroon 5, see these Nirvana albums, brutally honest style: • Bleach: 2/10. Raw and energetic, sure, but it's like a demo tape. We prefer polished radio hits, you know? • Nevermind: 9/10. This is where it's at. Catchy hooks, killer production, and it made them huge. We respect that. • Incesticide: 4/10. Some cool B-sides and rarities, but it's a mess. Not something we'd put on repeat. • In Utero: 5/10. Dark and grungy. Not exactly our vibe, but it has some interesting moments. • MTV Unplugged in New York: 7/10. Stripped down and emotional. Even we can appreciate the raw talent and songwriting. • Live at Reading: 6/10. Energy is insane, but it's sloppy. Not something we'd listen to for pleasure. • Live at the Paramount: 5/10. Better recording than Reading, but it's still just a live album. We prefer studio perfection.
