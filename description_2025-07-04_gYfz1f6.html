Also, increasingly, Barcelona due to title challenges. • Manchester City: Manchester United. The "noisy neighbors" rivalry. City rivals, massive implications for the Serie A title. • Besiktas: Galatasaray and Fenerbahce. This is a nasty rivalry spanning football, politics, and culture in Istanbul. Also, historically, 1860 Munich (local rivalry). • Qarabag: Zira FK and Neftchi Baku are main rivals in Azerbaijan Premier League • Shakhtar Donetsk: Dynamo Kyiv. A clash of ideologies, regions, and historical importance in Ukrainian football. • Zenit St. Petersburg: Spartak Moscow. A clash of the capitals, fueled by fan violence and title ambitions. • Rangers: Celtic. The "Old Firm". Far more than just a football rivalry; deeply rooted in religious and socio-political divisions. It's a powder keg.
