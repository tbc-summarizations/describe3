Wake me up when he starts rapping about something real. • Negus: 1/10. More like Negativity. No real songs? No thank you. You can't just put out snippets of something and call it a album and expect people to like it. Overall, this dude is straight basura. He needs to start making music that actually slaps. And while we are at it, stop trying to act so holier than thou. All of this is just my opinion though.
