Alright, let's tear into these tracks one by one. No holding back! 1. "The Creator Has a Master Plan" - Pharoah Sanders: This song is over half an hour long? Who has time for this meandering saxophone solo? I bet the creator's master plan was to bore listeners into submission. 2. "The Glowing Man" - Swans: Oh great, another Swans epic. I'm sure it's a masterpiece of droning noise, perfect for contemplating the meaninglessness of existence. 3. "Cloud of Unknowing" - Swans: So, Swans have a thing for long, brooding song titles, huh? The song probably sounds like what it feels like to be trapped in a fog of pretentious misery. 4. "Sleep" - Godspeed You Black Emperor!: Sleep? More like "Put Me to Sleep." I bet this track is just a bunch of build-ups that never pay off. 5. "Static" - Godspeed You Black Emperor!: I imagine this is just the musical equivalent of white noise. I bet it would be great as a sound for fixing a noisy PC. 6. "Storm" - Godspeed You Black Emperor!: I wonder if this song is just a series of cymbal crashes. A real storm of pretension. 7. "Frankie M" - Swans: I bet this song is garbage and that Frankie M should have stayed at the school he dropped out of. 8.
