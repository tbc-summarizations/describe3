Alright, check it, here's how Big Willy styles rates A Tribe Called Quest: • People's Instinctive Travels and the Paths of Rhythm: 7/10. It's got that fun, carefree vibe, but it's a little too all over the place for my taste. • The Low End Theory: 10/10. This album is a masterpiece. The beats, the rhymes, the chemistry – it's all perfect. I can listen to this from start to finish any day. • Midnight Marauders: 9/10. Almost as good as The Low End Theory. Smooth, jazzy, and lyrically on point. • Beats, Rhymes and Life: 6/10. It's alright, but Q-Tip and Phife Dawg weren't on the same page. Consequence was a good addition. • The Love Movement: 5/10. This is where they were starting to show that the energy was fading. • We got it from Here... Thank You 4 Your service: 8/10. Tribe went out on a high note. It's mature, thoughtful, and has some killer beats. It was the perfect goodbye.
