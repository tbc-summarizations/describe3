Belgium is a team living on reputation, clinging to a "golden generation" that has long since faded. The Dutch have better tactical structure and more ruthless finishing. Belgium's defense is creaky and their midfield lacks bite. Expect the Dutch to exploit those weaknesses with a convincing win. • Switzerland vs. Croatia: Croatia, but this one will be a slog. This match will be a painful lesson for Spain, as Germany clinically exposes their weaknesses. • France vs. Denmark: France, and it won't even be close. Denmark had their moment a few years ago.
